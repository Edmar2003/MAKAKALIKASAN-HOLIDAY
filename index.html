Our Teachers

Our teachers gave us guidance and care.

Sir. Flor

Practical Research 2

Focuses on quantitative research methods, teaching students how to design, conduct, and present research studies.

Sir. Niel

CPAR & 21st Century Literature

(CPAR) Explores the diversity and richness of Philippine arts from different regions, fostering appreciation for local artistic expressions and cultural heritage.

(21st CL) Examines literary works from the Philippines and around the world in the context of 21st century themes and digital media influences.

Sir. Makaraig

Understanding Culture, Society and Politics

Investigates human cultural variation, social differences, social change, and political identities to develop cultural awareness and sensitivity.

Sir. Torres

Physical Education and Health

Focuses on fitness, sports, and health education to promote lifelong wellness and physical activity.

Ma'am. Araneta

Physical Science

Covers basic concepts of chemistry and physics, including matter, motion, energy, and the universe.

Ma'am. Ravis

EAPP & MIL

(EAPP) Develops communication skills in academic and professional contexts, emphasizing critical reading and writing.

(MIL) Teaches how to access, analyze, evaluate, and create media content responsibly in the digital age.

Ma'am. Viana

Filipino sa Piling Larang (FPL)

Enhances Filipino language skills in specific fields or tracks, focusing on technical and professional applications.

Sir. Vincent

Business Finance

Introduces financial management principles, investment decisions, and financial planning for businesses.

Ma'am. Rochelle

Marketing & Business Ethics

Principles of Marketing: Covers fundamental marketing concepts, strategies, and consumer behavior in business contexts.

Business Ethics and Social Responsibility: Examines ethical decision-making and corporate social responsibility in business environments.

Ma'am. Ramos

FABM 2

Provides foundational knowledge in accounting, business, and management principles for ABM students.

Sir. Erik

Work Immersion

Provides hands on experience in real work environments to develop skills and prepare students for future careers.
